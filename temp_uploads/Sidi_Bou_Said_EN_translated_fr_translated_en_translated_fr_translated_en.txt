Sidi Bou Said - The Blue and White Jewellery of Tunisia Sidi Bou Said is a picturesque coastal town located only 20 kilometres from Tunis, the capital of Tunisia. Known for its distinctive blue and white architecture, it is perched on a cliff overlooking the Mediterranean Sea. The city is called Abu Said al-Baji, a 13th century Sufi saint who lived and died there. Its mausoleum remains a spiritual and cultural monument.
